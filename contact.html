Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find your answer in our FAQ section.

Q: How can I report a broken manga or missing chapters?

A: Use our contact email (contact@xbato.co.uk) and include the manga title, chapter number, and description of the issue.

Q: Can I request a specific manga to be added?

A: While we can't guarantee all requests, you can send suggestions to our general support email.

Q: How long does it typically take to get a response?

A: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.
